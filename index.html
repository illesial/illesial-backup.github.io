About Me

My name is Osman Chaudhary and I'm a Data Scientist and Mathematician. I got my PhD in Mathematics at Boston University in 2017 under Clarence Eugene Wayne. My fields of research in mathematics are Dynamical Systems and Partial Differential Equations. I have a particular interest in using invariant manifold theory to reduce the dimension of models representing real-world phenomena in fluid mechanics and mathematical biology. You can read some of my research publications at the bottom, check out some of my general Data Science projects here, or continue to see some election data and mapping resources.

  • An interactive voter contact map for a recent NY State Assembly campaign, based on data from DNC's VAN. Purple indicates many voters contacted, light blue / white indicates few voters contacted. Click each election district to see details.

  • A Python NYC Election Results Mapping tutorial.
  • A simple python module to reformat NYC Board of Elections results data. Useful for matching results to shapefiles.
