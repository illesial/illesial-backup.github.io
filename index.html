About Me

My name is Osman Chaudhary and I'm a Data Scientist and Mathematician. I got my PhD in Mathematics at Boston University in 2017 under Clarence Eugene Wayne. My fields of research in mathematics are Dynamical Systems and Partial Differential Equations. I have a particular interest in using invariant manifold theory to reduce the dimension of models representing real-world phenomena in fluid mechanics and mathematical biology. You can read some of my research publications below, or check out some of my Data Science projects here.
